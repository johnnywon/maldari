말 becomes words, as it is spoken.

Maldari (말다리, “a bridge of words”) sits in your meeting, listens to Korean from the mic or any app's audio, and writes English beneath it — line by line, while people are still talking. Every session is saved, locally and to your own private site.

01 · Korean in — RTZR

Recognition built for 한국어

Audio streams over a raw WebSocket to RTZR's sommers_ko — speech recognition built in Korea, for Korean, decoding in the meeting domain with your vocabulary keyword-boosted. Partial hypotheses appear while the sentence is still being spoken; finals lock with punctuation and number normalization. General-purpose STT mangles Korean business speech — this doesn't.

wss://openapi.vito.ai/v1/transcribe:streaming · model_name=sommers_ko · domain=MEETING

02 · English out — Claude

Translation with a memory

Each finalized line streams through Claude with the conversation so far as rolling context — so dropped Korean subjects resolve correctly and hedging survives: 검토해보겠습니다 stays “we'll look into it,” never “we will do it.” Filler (어, 음, 그) is skipped with a ∅ sentinel instead of narrated. Tokens land in the transcript as they're generated, and the system prompt is cached — a one-hour meeting costs cents.

POST api.anthropic.com/v1/messages · stream: true · cache_control: ephemeral

01

Any voice in the room ·말

Capture the microphone, all system audio, or one specific app — Zoom, Meet, Teams — with a right-click. Streaming Korean speech recognition tuned for meetings.

02

Translation that keeps its word ·결

Claude renders business Korean into natural English and preserves commitment level — “검토해보겠습니다” stays “we'll look into it,” never “we will do it.”

03

Nothing is ever lost ·다리

Every line is written to disk as it lands, and each session syncs to your own private Maldari site as dated markdown. A crash, a restart, a closed laptop — the meeting survives.

04

Yours, all the way down ·집

Open source. Runs on your Mac with your own API keys. Transcripts live in your Cloudflare account behind your password — no third party reads your meetings.

mic / system audio → RTZR streaming STT → Claude translation → live transcript → your private site

Maldari is a single SwiftPM app plus one Cloudflare Worker. Clone it, read every line, run it yourself. If a meeting ever misbehaves, structured diagnostics on disk tell you exactly what happened.
