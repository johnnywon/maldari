macosx · live meeting translation

말 becomes words, as it is spoken.

Maldari (말다리, “a bridge of words”) sits in your meeting, listens to Korean from the mic or any app's audio, and writes English beneath it — line by line, while people are still talking. Every session is saved, locally and to your own private site.

↓ Download for macosx View source on GitHub

free & open source · macosx 14.4+ · bring your own RTZR + Anthropic keys

RTZR · 한국어 STT

Korean audio streams to RTZR's sommers_ko — a model built in Korea, for Korean, and rated better-trained on the language than general-purpose speech engines. It hears the business speech, numbers, and names that other models mangle.

Claude · translation

Each finalized line streams through Claude with the conversation as rolling context, so dropped subjects resolve and hedging survives — 검토해보겠습니다 stays “we'll look into it.” The transcript reads the way the meeting was meant.
